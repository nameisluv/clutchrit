About Us

Learn more about us

ClutchRIT Esports is an esports club at Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT) in Bangalore, India that hosts competitive video game tournaments for students and members of the RIT community. The club aims to provide a fun and engaging environment for players of all skill levels to come together and enjoy playing their favorite games while also promoting healthy competition. ClutchRIT Esports typically hosts events on a regular basis and may also participate in larger intercollegiate or regional esports tournaments. This club is an opportunity for RIT students to showcase their gaming skills, connect with other gamers and represent the college in various esports competitions.
